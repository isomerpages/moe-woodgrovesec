**

The English Language and Literature Department aims to empower students into becoming empathetic communicators, discerning readers and creative inquirers of the English Language. Apart from this, it also aims to cultivate the love of the English Language as an aesthetic form and empower students to use language creatively for impact.

  

Subjects Offered at Lower Secondary

  

*   English Language
    
*   English Literature
    

  

Subjects Offered at Upper Secondary

  

*   English Language
    
*   English Literature (Elective)
    
*   English Literature (Pure)
    

  

\*Subject availability is subjected to change and might differ for each year.

  
**Key Programmes**

*   Queens Commonwealth Essay Competition
    
*   ICAS Competition
    
*   Unity Writer’s Festival
    
*   National Poetry Writing Competition
    
*   Words Go Round
    



\*Programmes are subjected to availability and might differ for each year.
