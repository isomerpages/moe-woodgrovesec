<p>Garrick's story is a testament of resilience and strength, and we're proud
to have a champion in life amongst us. In fact, many of us wouldn't have
known his life story because he's such a bundle of joy and energy in school.</p>
<p>Despite his mum's battle with cancer, he shines bright with his leadership
role in NCC, performing on stage for Woodgrove's Got Talent, and challenging
himself academically by taking subjects at a higher level. At the same
time, he juggles family responsibilities, school, and passions with a smile
- he's a true inspiration!</p>
<p>Remember that life's challenges are not meant to break us, but to make
us!</p>
